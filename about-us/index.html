About Us

We're on a mission to help education reach its full potential.

We are an exclusive Hyderabad-specific platform that brings junior colleges and students together where students and parents can make informed decisions on choosing the right college.

We started Scolage to address the enormous information gap between the students and colleges. Our sole aim is to significantly help students in making the right career decisions.

Our Story

Scolage was founded when students experienced significant difficulties in obtaining entrance to the appropriate junior colleges.

Our founder developed the concept a few years ago when he visited colleges for his sibling's admittance. He discovered that there are no resources and a lack of information to help him get into the correct college.

seeing there is an opportunity He developed this concept in order to give a smooth and exceptional college choosing process for students and parents.
